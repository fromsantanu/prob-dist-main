| Type           | Meaning                                                     | Key Difference                     |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Weak LLN**   | Sample mean **converges in probability** to population mean | Convergence in probability         |

### ğŸ’» **Python Example**
